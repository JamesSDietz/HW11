Cloudiness

No evident linear relationship is apparent between cloudiness of cities and their latitude location. However, it is worthy of note that there appear to be several "bands" of cloudiness that can be observed for groups of cities. On closer inspection of the figure, it appears that there are groups of cities that have cloudiness in the 90s percentage, the mid to upper 70s percentage, and around zero percentage and these appear not to relate to the latitude location of the cities in question.
